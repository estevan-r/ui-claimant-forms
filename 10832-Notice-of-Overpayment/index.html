To make a bank account or credit card payment online:

  • Sign into your MyUI+ account and choose "Make a Payment"

To make a credit card payment over the phone:

  • Contact Benefit Payment Control at 303-318-9035, (toll-free) 1-877-464-4622; when the virtual agent picks up please say “speak to an agent.”

To make a payment by mail, send your payment to:

  • P.O. Box 8789, Denver, CO 80201-8789
  • To assist with processing the payment, include your name and either your social security number or claimant ID, found at the top of your MyUI+ homepage or at the top of this document.
  • Please make any mailed payments out to Colorado Department of Labor & Employment (CDLE)

If you can't pay the full amount at this time:

Please log into your MyUI+ account to set up a payment arrangement.

  • From your home page, select Manage Debt from the left-hand menu, then select the Maintain Payment Plan option to set up your payment arrangement.

If you are otherwise eligible to receive future unemployment benefits:

We will take money out of your weekly payments to offset the overpayment until the amount that you owe is paid in full. If necessary, we may intercept your State and/or Federal tax refund to pay back your overpayment.

If have questions regarding your overpayment, or if you feel the amount is incorrect:

Contact Benefit Payment Control at 303-318-9035, (toll-free) 1-877-464-4622; when the virtual agent picks up please say, “speak to an agent.”

If you feel that you can't afford to pay the amount you owe:

You have the right to request a waiver of your overpayment.

To request a waiver, follow the steps below:

  • The fastest way to request a waiver is to request it in MyUI+.
    • From your home page, select Manage Debt from the left-hand menu, then select the View Overpayment History option.
    • On the right side of the screen under Overpayment History, click the link in “Select here to request waiver.”
  • If you are not able to fill out the online form, you can submit your waiver request by completing Sections I, II, and III of this form and returning it via mail or fax it to 303-318-9037. Your responses to all questions must be accurate and complete. Please sign and date the form and make a copy of this entire notice for your records.

You will receive a Notice of Determination in MyUI+ with a decision about whether your waiver request is approved.
