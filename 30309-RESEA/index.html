Dear [Claimant's First Name],

You have been selected for and must attend a mandatory Reemployment Services and Eligibility Assessment (RESEA) meeting. You must complete the RESEA program to remain eligible for Unemployment Insurance benefits.

RESEA is a partnership program between Unemployment Insurance and Colorado Workforce Centers that will help prepare you for your next career move. RESEA offers free career coaching with an Unemployment Insurance Reemployment Navigator, which includes personalized assistance, job skills assessment and labor market information.

What you must to do to remain eligible for unemployment benefits:

1

Log into MyUI+ and complete your RESEA enrollment on the RESEA tab in the application:

  • Watch a Workforce Center video (approx. 1.5 minutes)
  • Watch an RESEA Orientation video (approx. 3 minutes)
  • Complete an Individualized Reemployment Plan (IRP) (approx. 10 minutes)
  • Schedule your RESEA Personalized Service Meeting (approx. 3 minutes)
    • Your meeting will be held on a virtual platform of Google Meet or Zoom (up to 60 minutes)
  • Register with your local Workforce Center (if you have not already done so)
